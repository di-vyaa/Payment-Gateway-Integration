ABOUT US



UMEED is a not-for-profit organisation working in India for 20 years,
focusing on alleviating poverty and hardships faced due to disasters.
We do this through well planned and comprehensive projects in
health, education, livelihoods and disaster preparedness and response.
Our overall goal is the empowerment of victims and thier families leading to improvement in their lives and livelihoods.
